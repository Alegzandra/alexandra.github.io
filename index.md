# PsihoRo: Depression and Anxiety Romanian Text Corpus

## The first Romanian open-source corpus for mental health

![Mental Health](./image.png)

---

## 📖 About the Project

**PsihoRo** is the first open-source Romanian corpus specifically designed for research in **depression and anxiety detection**.

It aims to support the development of NLP models and tools focused on **mental health analysis in the Romanian language**, a currently under-resourced area.
